# Market Data Reliability & Risk Index

## 1. Data Risk Factors in Geospatial Analysis
- **Source Credibility:** Reliability of census and pedestrian traffic data sources.
- **Data Aging:** Risk associated with using outdated consumer behavior metrics.
- **Geospatial Precision:** Accuracy of point-of-interest mapping for potential restaurant sites.

## 2. Measurement and Refinement
- Formulation of a reliability score for each target zone based on data age and source confidence.
- Procedure documentation for quarterly data intake and risk assessment.
