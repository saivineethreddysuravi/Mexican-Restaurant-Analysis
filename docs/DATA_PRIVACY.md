# Data Governance & Privacy: Consumer Data

## Data Handling
All consumer sentiment data is anonymized at the source. No PII (names, emails) is stored within the analytical datasets.

## Quality Standards
- **Source Verification:** Only verified Yelp/Google review APIs used for sentiment analysis.
- **Outlier Removal:** Transactions exceeding 5 standard deviations from the mean are flagged for manual data quality audit.

## Compliance
Complies with CCPA regarding the handling of California-based consumer data in the market study.
