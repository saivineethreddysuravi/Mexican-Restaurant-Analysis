# Market Intelligence Governance: Consumer Behavior Analysis

## Data Lineage
- **Source**: Public sentiment data, transaction logs, and consumer video feedback.
- **Processing**: Python ETL with DAX-based modeling in Power BI.
- **Reporting**: Weekly market trend dashboards.

## Consumer Privacy
- **Anonymization**: All individual consumer identifiers are removed prior to trend analysis.
- **Compliance**: Adherence to standard market research data privacy regulations.
