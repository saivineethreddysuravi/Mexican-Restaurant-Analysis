# Business Requirement Document (BRD): Market Opportunity Analysis

## 1. Project Overview
The objective of this analysis is to identify untapped market segments for a new Mexican restaurant concept, focusing on consumer sentiment and competitive density.

## 2. Business Objectives
- Identify the top 3 geographical clusters with high demand but low competition.
- Determine the most preferred price point and service style (Quick Service vs. Casual Dining).
- Analyze sentiment drivers for 'Authentic' vs. 'Tex-Mex' cuisines.

## 3. Data Requirements
| Data Source | Description | Update Frequency |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Yelp API** | Competitor ratings and review counts | One-time / Monthly |
| **Census Data** | Demographic information and income levels | Annual |
| **Consumer Surveys** | Primary data on dining preferences | Ongoing |

## 4. Analytical Scope
- **Geographic:** 50-mile radius from the target metropolitan area.
- **Segment:** Families, Young Professionals, and Students.
- **Metrics:** Sentiment Score (1-5), Market Saturation Index, Projected Average Check.

## 5. Success Criteria
- Delivery of a 'Heat Map' showing optimal locations.
- Sentiment analysis identifying 'Vegan Mexican' as a high-growth niche.
- Final recommendation report presented to stakeholders.
